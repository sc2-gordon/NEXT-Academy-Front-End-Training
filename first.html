The Start of Class!

"That's one small quack for man, one giant quack for mankind"

-Captain Quack

Duck training is an essential part to any budding duck owners. Whether you have a black, white or even pink duck, one must first teach them the basics of duck life. Join us on this 10-week long adventure as we help you to grow closer to your quacky companions.

In this training course, you will learn the following:

  • Potty train your duck!
  • Duck walks: Fact or fiction?
  • Can bread really kill my duck?

Basic Package

In the basic package, you get access to the essentials for all your duck training needs such as your leash and collar!

Advanced Package

In the advanced package, you get everything in the basic package along with your very own automatic duck feeder! Be better by doing less!
